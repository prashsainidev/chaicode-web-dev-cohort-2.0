## What I Learned

### Salary Negotiation Thinking

- understanding that negotiation is about clarity and positioning, not only asking for more money
- thinking about compensation in a broader way beyond just fixed salary
- learning how to approach offer discussions with better preparation and confidence

---

### Offer Evaluation

- comparing offers more carefully instead of reacting only to headline numbers
- thinking about role quality, growth, learning, and long-term direction
- understanding why the first offer should be reviewed strategically before accepting

---

### First 30 Days Mindset

- treating the first month after joining as an important setup phase
- focusing on communication, ownership, and learning speed after accepting an offer
- preparing for a smoother transition from interview success to actual execution at work
