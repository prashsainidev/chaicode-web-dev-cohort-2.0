# T11 - Resume Building & ATS Mastery

## Overview

This T-Class was held on Thursday, `09 Apr 2026`.

The topic for this session was `Resume Building & ATS Mastery`.

This was a non-coding career session led by HR ma'am. The focus was on improving resume quality, making the resume easier for recruiters and ATS systems to read, and understanding how to present experience, skills, and projects more clearly.

---

## What I Learned

### Resume Structure

- keeping the resume clear, focused, and role-relevant
- presenting education, skills, projects, and experience in a readable order
- avoiding unnecessary visual clutter in a technical resume

---

### ATS Awareness

- understanding that many companies use ATS systems before human screening
- using simple formatting that is easier for parsing tools
- keeping keywords relevant to the role without keyword stuffing

---

### Recruiter-Friendly Writing

- writing project and experience points with clear impact
- making the resume easy to scan quickly
- avoiding vague claims and focusing on concrete work
